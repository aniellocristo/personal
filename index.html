DEFINE THE PROBLEM

Employees were frustrated with keeping track of their projects on several different platforms, in several different locations. The primary issue requiring a resolution was to improve the method for task delivery. Timing and ease of task delivery would be the metric.

+

-

CONDUCT RESEARCH

Research invloved interviewing employees and observing daily routines. This process revealed employee's navigating between Box.com, two independant Microsoft Share Point interfaces, Plan Grid, Excel Files and Outlook Calendar. Employee's spent an average of 12 miniutes every hour navigating between program and file locations. In addtion to these 12 miniutes, employees spent an average of 5 minuites preforming a single given task, and then another average of 5 miniutes duplicating the same task for a different program.

ABOUT ME

I design mobile first, responsive website using HTML 5 and CSS3. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge of JavaScript and in the process I have already worked with technologies such as jQuery, JSON, and multiple public API’s. I believe in the beauty of simplicity, as it applies to all aspects of web design and programing. I value efficiency as much as creativity and favor legible and reusable code in such a way that it is easy to scale, maintain and debug. My formal educational background is Architecture. It was in that program I learned the very fundamentals of design that transcend all disciplines of creativity.
